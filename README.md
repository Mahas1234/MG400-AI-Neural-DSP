### **Bridging the gap between Human Vision and Digital Signal Processing.**

**MG400 AI** is not just a patch editor; it is a **Neural DSP Orchestrator**. By leveraging Google's **Gemini 2.5 Flash** and a proprietary **Cloud Mesh architecture**, we've transformed the NUX MG-400 into a high-tech lab for sonic experimentation. Describe your vision, and our AI synthesizes the entire signal chain in milliseconds.

## 🚀 The "Neural" Edge

*   **🧠 Cognitive Topology Synthesis:** Don't fiddle with knobs. Describe a mood—*"A high-gain lead that sounds like rain in a cyberpunk city"*—and our engine reconfigures every CMP, AMP, and EQ block to match the sonic DNA.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack & Architecture

- **Core Engine:** Python 3.12 + PySide6 (High-performance Desktop Core)
- **Intelligence:** Google Gemini 2.5 Flash (Large Language Model for DSP Mapping)
- **Real-time:** Pusher / Pysher (Worldwide Pub/Sub Event Synchronization)
- **Web Interface:** Next.js 16 + Framer Motion (Optimized for ultra-low latency mobile touch)
- **Hardware Bridge:** Bi-directional MIDI over USB/OTG
